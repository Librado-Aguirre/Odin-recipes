Los Tres Golpes

Los Tres Golpes, Strong Dominican Breakfast

Los Tres Golpes is a very popular dish consisting of mashed green plantains, usually served with Dominican sauteed red onions with vinegar, and with a side of fried eggs, fried Dominican salami, and Queso Frito (fried cheese). Served together, they are known as Los Tres Golpes (The Three Strikes).

Ingredients

  • Platain
  • Cheese, AKA Queso de freir
  • Salami
  • Eggs

Cooking Steps

Ingridients are cooked separately and served togueter

  1. Boil plantains

    2. Platain is prepared by boiling it in water, add salt to taste, when platain are completely cooked, match them with your prefer oil or butter. tip: use the garlik matching stick to get a sutil garlik taste.

  2. Cheese

    3. Fry cheese until it gets a light golden color. Cheese has to be a an special kind wich is suitable to fry.

  3. Salami

    4. Fry the salami to your prefered consistency

  4. Eggs

    5. Eggs are prefered fried, get your prefered consistency

  5. Serve

    6. serve separated or all withing a dish, accommodate each food on order way, letting each one show its colors.
